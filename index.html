Hi there! Welcome to Monthly Moments! My name is Isabelle Nguyen, creator of this website!
Now you may be wondering... what is Monthly Moments?

Monthly Moments is a video blog aka a vlog project that is meant to document the many special memories 2025 has in store for me!
Each month I will be filming small mini vlogs and taking photos whenever I am out and about so that by the end of the year I can look
back and see what all has happened. At the end of each year, I will invite my friends to watch a year end compilation that will feature
them and all the amazing memories we’ve made!

It’ll be fun!
Everything on this was made by yours truly :) ME IZZY
So have fun watching and looking through my life as you interact with this website, which was made by ME!!
